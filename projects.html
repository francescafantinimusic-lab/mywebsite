Solo

Duration: 40 minutes circa. Instrumentation: alto saxophone and/or tenor saxophone. Programmes: 1. Free improvisation solo: Bodies of sound explores the body as a source for improvisation and as a site of knowledge. This focus allows a decolonised approach to performance. The saxophone's body is also investigated in its components, at the search of more raw sounds.

2. Interpretation and improvisation: Embedded sounds. The project interwines compositions by Berio, Nono, Calzada, with free improvisation. The aim is to explore the time as a continuum, between kairos and kronos.

Booking: francesca.fantini.music@gmail.com

airkeychains trio

Duration: 40 minutes, one set or two sets. Instrumentation: saxophone soprano/alto, saxophone alto/tenor, euphonium. Programme: free improvisation, contemporary improvisation. The trio performes within the sonic enviroment of contemporary chamber music, freely improvising. Listening is shaping the practice and the performance. Booking: francesca.fantini.music@gmail.com

European Music Collective

Active collaborator of the international ensemble "European Music Collective". The ensemble focuses on collaborative creation, experimental notation, and transnational artistic exchange. Booking: europeanmusiccollective@gmail.com

Che genere di musica ascolti?

Talk, guidata dalla giornalista Elisa Belotti, intorno ai temi degli stereotipi di genere in musica, partendo dalla figura di Elise Hall, pionera del saxofono. Segue concerto in duo di sax, con Martina Madini (sax soprano) e Francesca Fantini (sax tenore). Booking: francesca.fantini.music@gmail.com
