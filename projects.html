Solo

Duration: 40 minutes circa. Instrumentation: alto saxophone and/or tenor saxophone. Programme: 1. Free improvisation solo: Bodies of sound/. it explores the body as a source for improvisation. The body is intended as a site of knowledge. This focus allows a decolonised approach to performance. The saxophone's body is also taken and investigated in its parts: the solo researches ancestral sounds through the use of only the saxophone neck or the whole saxophone without mouthpiece. 2. Interpretation and improvisation: Embedded sounds. Luciano "Berio Sequenxa IX B", Luigi Nono "Djamila Boupacha", Mario Calzada "Colors and Forms", with free improvisation intersections. The aim is to explore the time as a continuum, as kairos, as kronos. Booking: francesca.fantini.music@gmail.com
